about us

Welcome to our tattoo salon, where we believe that tattoos are not just ink on skin, but a form of self-expression and art.

Our team of experienced and talented tattoo artists are dedicated to creating unique and personalized designs for our clients, ensuring that each tattoo is a reflection of their personality and story.

services

Our salon is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and maintains the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene to ensure the safety of our clients.
Size tattoo

The price of a tattoo depends on the size. Everything is decided individually from the picture. Here are the minimum prices:

XS / S - min. $50

M / L - min. $150

XL - min. $250

Placement

We make tattoos on any part of the body. But you have to consider the pain threshold of each place.

The most painless - are biceps, on the forearms and on the lower leg

The most painful - are the head and neck, the elbow, the back of the hand, the shoulder blade, the knee, the ribs, and the feet.
